What happens when my trial ends?

If your trial ends before you choose a plan, your account will freeze. Your data will remain intact, though inaccessible, until you choose a plan and add a credit card.

How can I pay for iRecognize?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe.

What are the technical requirements for using iRecognize?

iRecognize is available anywhere you have an internet connection. There's nothing to install, and all you need is a modern web browser.
